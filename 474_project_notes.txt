Afou dimiourgisa ena package game kai mia klasi BrickBreaker.
Deksia sto properties tou .fla arxeiou, evala sto class tin klasi 
game.BrickBreaker. Einai i klasi pou kaleite otan treksoume to paixnidi (ctrl + enter)

Sti sunexeia prepei na pareis copy paste apo to allo project ena symbol 
kai na to valeis se kapoio layer. 

Opou uparxei kati = new kati() einai ta plaina sprites, ta kalei gia na 
ta valei panw sto stage.

To kathe symbol apoteleitai apo 1 , 2 h' parapanw symbols , px gia to paddle einai to paddle kai to paddlegf
gia to startgame einai to start kai to symbol 1 copy121, sta deksia tou fainetai i 
klasi stin opoia mpainoun.
